UX Project: Vice Perspectives Chatbot

Vice Perspectives Overview

US Case Study // Fall 2017

For a senior UX design class, I selected, researched, and designed a digital solution and pitch for VICE News. To address a clear business problem, my team and I designed a chatbot that delivers first-hand, journalistic news through a chatbot interface.
