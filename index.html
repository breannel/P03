Styleguide

The Client

Metropolis Comics and Toys is a local Burnaby shop that sells gundams, graphic novels, statues, and figures. They were established almost a decade ago and are known for their personable service and open-concept brick and mortar - perfect for community events. After conducting a 10 question survey and interviewing the client, the data was used as the basis for most of the websites design decisions.

Values

  • Approachable: friendly, accepting, open
  • Laidback: casual, informal, easy going
  • Supportive: community-minded, brick and mortar, always available
  • Respectful: act with integrity, treat customers how we'd like to be treated
  • Honest: reliable, trustworthy, tell it like it is

Branding

Logo

The logo was created by Michael Cohen in 2011, it was inspired by retro-futurism, Art Nouveau, and Art Deco. It is equal parts inpiration from Metropolis city, where Superman hails from, and Metropolis the 1927 film.
