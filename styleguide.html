Branding

Values and Character

My new portfolio will highlight and be guided by cultural background as a 4th generation Chinese-Canadian, partly by highlighting and reclaiming the neon aesthetic that was banned by the Canadian government in a direct attack against chinese owned businesses. Below is a picture of the shop that belonged to my family for 3 generations before we were forced to leave the dying neighbourhood. My tone and general values here include:

  • Cultural Duality: worldly, nostalgic, respectful
  • Dweeby: nerdy, weaboo-esque, beat of my own drum
  • Frank: sincere, honest, tell it like it is
