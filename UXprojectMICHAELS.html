UX Project: Michaels Crafting Studio

Michaels Crafting Studio

For a senior UX design class, I selected, researched, and designed a digital solution and pitch for the crafting supply chain Michaels. To address a competitive market where Michaels is falling behind in digital sales, my team and I designed a crafting studio that draws customers both in-store and online.

Project Length

Design Overview

Michaels Crafting Studio is an experience that lives on a new section the Michaels website. It stores and suggests crafts to visitors, be they a wary beginner or a veteran crafter. Each craft is packaged in a kit with all necessary materials and can be picked up in-store off the shelf or pre-ordered online. The crafter can then book a space at the in-store studio to borrow tools, or take it straight home.
