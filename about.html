So Why the Neon?

In the 1960's, my family owned the Honk Kong Cafe on East Pender, and our street was knwon for being the most neon street in the whole world at the time. With the intention of targeting chinese-owned business, the governemnt outlawed neon signs. I'm reclaiming the aesthetic as my own.
